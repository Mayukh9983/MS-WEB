Our Courses

A comprehensive list of courses designed for your success.

ACCT (Accountancy)

Master the language of business. This course covers the fundamentals of financial accounting, from recording transactions to preparing financial statements, ensuring a strong foundation for future commerce studies and careers.

CSTX (Costing & Taxation)

Delve into the essential principles of cost management, cost control techniques, and the intricacies of the Indian tax system, including direct and indirect taxes. This course prepares students for practical applications in business and finance.

CLPA (Commercial Law and Preliminaries of Auditing)

Gain a strong foundation in the legal framework governing businesses, contracts, and various commercial transactions. Additionally, explore the basic principles of auditing and assurance, understanding their importance in financial governance and transparency.

BSTD (Business Studies)

Explore the nature and purpose of business, various forms of business organizations, and the social responsibilities of business in a dynamic economic environment. This course provides a holistic view of business operations and management principles.
